Facilities

  • Cisco Networking Academy

    Cisco Networking Academy transforms the lives of learners, educators and communities through the power of technology, education and career opportunities. Available to anyone, anywhere.

    Our ongoing partnership with Cisco Networking Academy is based on our need to keep a robust employee pipeline as we focus on ‘new in career’ talent.

  • Internet of Things Lab

    Our college provibes an IOT lab (Internet Of Things) with newest hardwares and softwares helping students to learn the core concepts of IOT and use its advantages they want.

    Lab has various tupes of mini and microcomputers including latest raspberry pi and adrino to enable students to learn.

  • Ubuntu Computer Center

    Campus has a Ubuntu computer centre with more then 150 high performence computers consisting latest generation Intel Chipsets and to give students best performence and stay productive.

    Labs are also equiped with hi-speed internet access to help students learn.

  • Mac Computer Center

    Students should not learn only one type of technology so, we established MAC lab to help students hands on apple infrastructure to widen their knowledge of computers systems and platforms.

    This lab provides a high speed, next gen computing enviroment which delivers fast performence to students and help them learn a new platform.
